UberDelta is an decentralized exchanged built upon the Ethereum blockchain launching in February 2018.

Our publically available and independently-audited smart contract includes features such as:
  • Lower gas consumption than other major decentralized exchanges
  • Complete security of user funds as user remains in total control of deposit/withdraw functionality
  • Built-in upgrade functionality
  • Low trading fees and increased discounts during launch period
  • Supports leading token definitions: ERC20, ERC233, and more
Additional features of the exchange:
  • Fast off-chain orderbook service
  • Dedicated support team
